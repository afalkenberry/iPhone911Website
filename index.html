About Us



The only locally owned and operated iPhone repair store in the city of Jacksonville. We repair all things Apple.



What we do

Specializing in the repair of Apple products, we have mastered the iDevice platform using the highest quality parts we are able to offer at affordable prices so that we can deliver the highest quality service and experience.

We differ from other smartphone repair shops in Jacksonville in that we strive to provide our customers with both the best quality repairs and service. Not dominate the market. We opened our doors in 2010 being one of the first iPhone repair shops in Jacksonville, and we are the only remaining locally owned, non-franchised store proudly maintaining a nearly 100% customer satisfaction.

90-day warranty

All our repairs come with an above the industry-standard 90-day warranty against defective parts. If you aren't satistied with the parts or service you received from us, you will receive a replacement at no cost. Our warranty covers all defective parts installed by us. Does not include damage to parts ie, cracked screens or water damage on the installed parts.
